    Hindu Institute of Management


    The Hindu Institute of Management was established in the year 1994 by The Sonepat Hindu Educational and Charitable Society, Sonepat. The Institute is providing instructions in two year full time M.B.A and three year full time M.C.A programmes (two years MCA full time lateral entry) and also three year full time program (B.B.A and B.C.A). These programmes are approved by AICTE, Government of India and are affiliated to DCRUST, Murthal. The Institute is located just opposite the Sonepat Railway Station and is also close to the Bus Stand. This makes the Institute easily accessible from Delhi and nearby towns.
